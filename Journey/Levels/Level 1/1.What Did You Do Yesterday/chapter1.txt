----> A Day Out

Mark went to the swimming pool yesterday.

He went with his father and his sister, Cathy.

He can swim but his sister cannot.

Donna was not happy yesterday afternoon.

She hurt her arm on the slide at the park.

The nurse took an X-ray.

Donna's brother Martin didn't go to the hospital.

He went to the train station to meet his Uncle Alan.

John went to the shops yesterday.

First he went to the post office.

He saw his friend Steve.

Kate went to the zoo with her friend Rachel yesterday.

They saw lions, elephants, koalas, and monkeys.

Kate gave a banana to a monkey.

Maria didn't go to the zoo yesterday, she went to the library.

She likes to read.

She took home five books!
